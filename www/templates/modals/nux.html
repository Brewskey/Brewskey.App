Welcome to Brewskey

Brewskey keeps track of every pour on your keg. View graphs or leaderboards to see who poured, when they poured, and how much. Every pour offers a chance to unlock a new drinking.

How to pour

  1. Click the pour button in the app
  2. You'll see a popup
  3. Enter the 6-digit code. The code is displayed on the Brewskey box
  4. The Brewkey box should turn green
  5. You can now pour from your keg!
